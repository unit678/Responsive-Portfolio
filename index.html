Where The Journey Begins!

A little bit about me!

Hello, my name is Garrion Martin and I received my Bachelor’s degree from Albany State University in Criminal Justice, also while being a full time student I was on a full scholarship in Track and Field.

I have always been interested in computers growing up, as I was growing up I saw computer languages, it just always looked hard.

A friend of mine I grew up with once called me telling me about coding and I felt it was a sign to pursue my original dreams because I never talked about having a passion for learning computer languages. I started looking on YouTube on computer languages and I never stopped looking. I started small classes that allowed me to be ready for the Georgia Tech coding entry exam.

I am pursuing my next career in becoming a Full Stack Developer at Georgia Tech. I am being challenged more than I have ever been and I’m looking forward to changing the world.
